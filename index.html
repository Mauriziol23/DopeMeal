Made with Love

While working at the headquarters of a major grocer, I received a sample of overnight oats that I thought my son would like. So, like any person who likes free stuff, I brought the sample home, and as my child read the package he frowned at the flavor and asked if we could make our own. I shrugged and said “sure!” We gathered our ingredients and then got to work. Smelling excitedly along the way, we then put the oats in the fridge and went on with our day. The next morning, he opened the container and said “wow Mom, this smells so good! He excitedly took a bite, wiggled his head and then smiled. He said “Mom, I think we’re on to something” and asked if he could save some for his best friend; once again I agreed. After his friend tried our oats, he bounces back and forth and says “Ms. Darian, this is so good, you could sell it!” That was the start of Oatmeal & Company…

Oatmeal & Company is not your basic oatmeal. The “& Company” represents the combination of organic rolled oats and the amazing flavors that we’ve thoughtful paired together. Made in my kitchen with an immense amount of love and pride. It is my sincerest hope that you’ll enjoy every bite.

Many Thanks,


Darian, Mother of Dane and Owner of Oatmeal & Company
